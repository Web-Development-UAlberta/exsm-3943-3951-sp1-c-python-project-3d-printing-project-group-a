# TEAM NORMS
### **Communication and Collaboration Methods:**
- Team Communication: 
  - Discord Group
  - Whatsapp Group

- Direct Communication:
  - Discord (Preferred)
  - Whatsapp
  - Text Message (Not preferred)
  - Email (Preferred)
- Team Collaboration: 
  - Github
  - Google Docs
  - Google Drive
  - Discord Group
  - Email

### **Punctuality & Attendance:**
- Team members should meet at least once a week to discuss project items
- Team meetings will be scheduled most convenient to team members schedules
- Team members are expected to participate in group discussions
- Team members should not be late or no-show to team meetings
- Team members should be responsive through team communication

### **Meeting Times, Location & Management:**
- Meeting times will be scheduled between 6PM-9PM MST (Weekdays/Weekends)
- Team members will meet on Monday before presentation to the Client
- Team members can decide on a meeting date most convenient to everyone
- Team members should be aware of each others schedule (Work, recreational, etc)
- Team members will decide the best location/method to collaborate

### **Expectations:**
- Project will be implemented through team collaboration
- Each group member is expected to agree to a reasonable portion of work
- Team members will be assigned to tasks most strongest to their expertise
- All work should be reviewed before main branch commitment

### **Conflict Resolution:**
- Conflicts between issues/roadblocks will be discussed and resolved with confidence
- Everyone’s opinion matters and taken into consideration
- A vote between team members can be used to select resolution

### **Strikes:**
- Personal attacks will result in a strike
- Refusal to partake in their share of work will result in a strike
- Team acknowledgement for team member use of AI will result in a strike

### **Member Removal Policy:**
- Team member will be presented to the instructor with evidence of relevant infraction(s) by the remaining members of the group
- The instructor will decide the course of action
